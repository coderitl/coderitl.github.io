+ 推荐`Anaconda`安装
+ 为`Pycharm`使用`anaconda`作为`python`解释器

###  去除Pycharm的各种波浪线

```bash
File --> Setting --> Editor --> Colors Scheme --> General

右侧区域: Errors and Warnings
```
